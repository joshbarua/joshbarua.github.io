Current local minima
Reasoning in LLMs
Recently, I've been exploring the underlying principles of reasoning in LLMs: to what extent it generalizes, how we can control it, and what the role of human-like reasoning is in model computation.

Science of language models
I am interested in understanding the structure of representations and the kinds of solutions neural networks learn. Can we find simple explanations in small models and controlled testbeds that reliably predict more complex phenomena?
