Data Science Discovery, 2023

A Recommender System Based on Customer Preferences and Product Reviews
Josh Barua
Towards Data Science, 2020
[article]

I propose a recommender system that matches consumers to products based on product reviews and a users perferred features in a product. The system doesn't rely on a users purchase history, and thus bypasses the cold start problem and preserves consumer privacy.
Who is Your Competitor in the Era of the Long Tail?
Josh Barua
DataDrivenInvestor, 2020
[article]

I show that recommender systems can dramatically increase the diversity of suggested products, and thus intensify rivalry between manufacturers who were not direct competitors in a world where consumers had to search for and evaluate products all by themselves.
