Bidirectional Captioning for Clinically Accurate and Interpretable Models
Keegan Quigley, Miriam Cha, Josh Barua, Geeticka Chauhan, Seth Berkowitz, Steven Horng Polina Golland
Arxiv, 2023
[paper]

We present optimal architecture and hyperparameters for RadTex, extending the contributions of VirTex for data-efficient pretraining to the radiology domain. We show that bidirectional captioning pretraining is competitive with contrastive image-text pretraining for learning strong visual representations. We demonstrate RadTex's capability to generate clinically accurate radiology reports from flexible user-driven prompts.
