Hey there! I'm a research associate at UT Austin, advised by Kyle Mahowald. I'm drawn to ideas that develop a deep understanding of the underlying mechanisms, capabilities, and limitations of language models. Recently, I've been thinking about how we can develop reasoning models that are flexible and generalize well out-of-distribution.
